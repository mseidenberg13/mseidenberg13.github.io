  • My First Python Project

The purpose of this project was to use python, pandas, matplotlib, and API's to prove or disprove a hypothesis about a certain topic. Our group choose to analize accident data the past 3 years in the US, to see if there were any conditions that increased accident likelyhood. We found that when more people are on the road more accidents happen. For example, during rush hour, in very populated areas, and during common weather conditions.
